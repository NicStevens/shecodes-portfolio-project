About me

My name is a Nicky Stevens. I am a soon-to-be graduate of SheCode's coding bootcamp, a Front-end Developer, and an Information Management Professional based in Florida, USA. With a Bachelor degree in Film, a minor in history, and a Masters in Library and Information Science, making information available in creative, organized, and user-friendly ways is a passion of mine.

I have experience using multiple markup and programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, manipulating data, and using APIs and AI technology. In addition, I have been managing information in the library and archives fields for 14 years. Through the years I have honed my skills in information management, databases, reference services, research, education, and customer service making sure users, whether public citizens, corporate customers, academic researchers, or professional colleauges, have the information they need.
